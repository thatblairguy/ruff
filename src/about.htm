The Ruff Edges Sweet Purrs Pet Bakery and Boutique was founded out of a desire to give our pets healthier treats. Just healthy, simple ingredients go into our biscuits. Every treat we bake is wheat, corn and soy free and baked in small batches. We use cage free eggs and add no fillers. We thought everyone should have wholesome, healthy food. Dogs know the difference, we're certain of it. So try them out.

A second focus of our business is our desire to provide employment opportunities to special needs adults. Currentlyu, two people are helping with the baking and packaging. As the business grows, we hope to increase this number.
